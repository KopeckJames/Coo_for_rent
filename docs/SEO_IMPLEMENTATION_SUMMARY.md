## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Keyword stuffing** - Use keywords naturally, not excessively
2. **Buying backlinks** - Google will penalize you
3. **Duplicate content** - Always create original content
4. **Ignoring mobile** - 60%+ of searches are mobile
5. **Slow page speed** - Google prioritizes fast sites
6. **Inconsistent NAP** - Use same business info everywhere
7. **No local focus** - Local SEO is crucial for service businesses
8. **Giving up too soon** - SEO takes 3-6 months to show results
9. **Not tracking metrics** - You can't improve what you don't measure
10. **Ignoring user intent** - Create content that helps your audience
