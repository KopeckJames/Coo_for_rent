## üîç Keyword Usage Guidelines

### On-Page SEO Best Practices

#### Title Tags (60 characters max)
- Include primary keyword
- Add location if relevant
- Make it compelling
- Examples:
  - "Fractional COO Texas | 25+ Years Experience | COO for Rent"
  - "Interim COO Services | Expert Operations Leadership"
  - "Part-Time COO for Scaling Businesses | Texas"

#### Meta Descriptions (155 characters max)
- Include primary keyword
- Include secondary keyword
- Add call-to-action
- Examples:
  - "Hire a fractional COO in Texas with 25+ years experience. Expert in operations, field service & construction. Get systems that scale. Free consultation."
  - "Interim COO services for growing businesses. Build operational infrastructure, lead teams, drive results. 3-6 month engagements. Texas-based."

#### H1 Tags (One per page)
- Include primary keyword
- Make it compelling
- Examples:
  - "Fractional COO Services in Texas"
  - "Interim COO: Expert Operational Leadership"
  - "Part-Time COO for Scaling Service Companies"

#### H2/H3 Tags
- Include secondary keywords
- Use natural language
- Structure content logically
- Examples:
  - "What is a Fractional COO?"
  - "Benefits of Hiring a Part-Time COO"
  - "When Your Business Needs Interim COO Services"

#### Body Content
- Keyword density: 1-2%
- Use keywords naturally
- Include variations and synonyms
- Focus on user intent
- Answer questions directly

#### Image Alt Text
- Describe the image
- Include keywords when relevant
- Keep it concise
- Examples:
  - "Fractional COO consulting with Texas business owner"
  - "Operations consultant reviewing field service dispatch system"
  - "Interim COO leading team meeting"

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Focus on intent, not just volume** - A keyword with 50 searches/month and high intent is better than 500 searches with low intent

2. **Target question keywords** - "How", "what", "when", "why" keywords often have lower competition and higher conversion

3. **Don't ignore local** - For a service business, "fractional COO Dallas" is more valuable than generic "fractional COO"

4. **Use keyword variations** - Don't just target "fractional COO", also target "part-time COO", "interim COO", "COO for hire"

5. **Track competitor keywords** - See what's working for competitors and create better content

6. **Update old content** - Refresh and optimize existing pages for new keywords

7. **Think like your customer** - What would they search for when they need your services?
